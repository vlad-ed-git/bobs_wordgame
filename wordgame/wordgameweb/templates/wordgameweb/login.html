Hello and welcome to wordgame's website.

This tool helps teachers create english word lists for the android app WordGame by Bob.
  1. Sign up now (Or login to your account)
  2. Create word lists and receive a unique identifier for your lists
  3. Provide the unique identifier to your students so they can access your lists.
  4. Track your students progress from your dashboard.

To learn more about the app, feel free to contact us (scroll to bottom for contacts).
